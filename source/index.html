Cruises to Antarctica

The magical world of icebergs and penguins

Travel to the ends of the earth

This magical journey will help you get to know the wonderful world of Antarctica. Our cruise ship will take you to the ends of the Earth, where you can see: icebergs, several types of penguins, whales, fur seals, and much more.

Benefits

Small a cruise ship

A cruise ship with just 300 guests aboard can help you avoid noise and queues. And will give you an opportunity to enjoy the views and nature.

All inclusive

All our cruises operate according to the "All-Inclusive" system. Great dishes and an unlimited bar available for you 24 hours.

Excursions by Zodiac

We offer excursion tours to the shores of Antarctica on equipped Zodiac boats with experienced guides.
